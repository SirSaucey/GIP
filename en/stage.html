Internship


I recently had the opportunity to participate in the Erasmus program and travel to Italy for my internship. It was an amazing experience that allowed me to not only learn about working in a different country, but also immerse myself in a new culture and environment. I was placed in a small town in the east of Italy, close to San Marino where I worked at ....
I was initially nervous about the language barrier and cultural differences, but my colleagues were incredibly welcoming and patient with me as I adjusted. One of the biggest differences I noticed was the work-life balance in Italy. The pace of work was slower and there was a greater emphasis on taking breaks and enjoying lunch with colleagues. It was a refreshing change from the fast-paced work environment I was used to.

Overall, my Erasmus experience in Italy was an unforgettable one, it was a great opportunity to learn about a new culture, improve my language skills and gain valuable work experience. I feel incredibly grateful for the opportunity and would highly recommend the program to anyone considering it.
