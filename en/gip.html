My GIP Project

Why?

I had to choose between three ideas for my GIP project and those ideas were: a 3D Horror Game, a 2D Hack & Slash Dungeon Game, and a language App. I chose the 3D Horror Game for my GIP not only because I am fond of the horror genre and quite enjoy it, but also because I like a challenge. The game is currently in development and I have made some nice progress on it, but I still feel that it is not enough and I want to do more to make it even better.

About the game

As the creator of this 3D horror game, I have tried to craft an intense and captivating experience for players. From the moment they crash their car in the forest, players are immediately thrown into a eerie and mysterious environment. As they follow the path deeper into the forest, they will discover a house and a shed, adding to the sense of uncertainty and danger. The inclusion of lore and a drawing featuring the monster hunting them, "Mr. Boogie," adds a layer of backstory and depth to the game, making the player feel like they are uncovering the secrets of the forest. The objective of collecting 8 mannequin body parts adds a sense of urgency and danger to the player's journey through the abandoned structures and hideout. All of these elements come together to create a thrilling and engaging game that will keep players on the edge of their seats.

Conclusion

I am enjoying the process of creating my own 3D horror game for my GIP Project, but also feel overwhelmed by the amount of work that comes with being a single developer. One solution could be to consider working on smaller or 2D games in the future, which may require less time and effort to complete.
