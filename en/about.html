So, who am I?

With a strong work ethic and a love for technology, Rares is determined to make a meaningful impact in the field of computer science.
He is interested in programming and web development & design and hopes to become a full-stack developer later.
He came up with 3 ideas for his school project: a 3D Horror Game, which he ultimately chose to work on, a 2D Hack & Slash Dungeon Game, and a Language App. Rares-Eduard's hobbies include playing games, going out with friends and playing football.
